## 🚀 Project Overview
[cite_start]**FreshConnect** is a technology-driven ecosystem developed to solve two critical agricultural problems: **poor market access for farmers** and **inefficient management of agricultural waste**[cite: 31, 47]. [cite_start]By leveraging a Flutter-based mobile application with a robust Firebase backend, we provide a direct-to-consumer marketplace while facilitating the sale of bio-waste to recycling companies for renewable energy production[cite: 36, 59, 60].

## 😟 The Problem Statement
Traditional agricultural supply chains are plagued by:
* [cite_start]**Lack of Market Access:** Farmers cannot reach consumers directly, leading to dependence on middlemen and low market prices[cite: 31, 47].
* [cite_start]**Unfair Profit Distribution:** Middlemen profit margins are significantly higher than the farmer's own margin[cite: 34].
* **Waste Mismanagement:** Farmers are often unaware of the market demand for raw materials produced from bio-waste[cite: 42, 45].
* [cite_start]**Economic Strain:** Most earnings are reinvested in cultivation and pesticides, leaving inadequate income for daily living[cite: 40, 48].

---

## 💡 Our Solution: The FreshConnect App
[cite_start]FreshConnect acts as a "Connector" that optimizes the supply chain to benefit both the producer and the end-user[cite: 64, 72].

### 1. Direct Marketplace
[cite_start]By removing middlemen, we increase the **Farmer's Margin** while simultaneously reducing the **Consumer Selling Price**[cite: 51, 53, 62].
* [cite_start]**Impact:** In our use case, the middleman-led price of 80 is reduced to 65 for consumers, while the farmer's profit rises from 30 to 40[cite: 66, 67, 70, 71].
* **Efficiency:** After FreshConnect involvement, retail prices dropped by an average of over 15%, and farmgate prices increased by nearly 10%[cite: 261].

### 2. Bio-Waste Valorization
The app allows farmers to sell agricultural waste directly to recycling companies[cite: 56, 60].
* [cite_start]**Renewable Energy:** Waste is repurposed for biogas plants and bio-slurry production[cite: 18, 27].
* [cite_start]**Sustainable Output:** Agricultural waste like rice straw and corn stover is processed via biorefineries to create biofuels, bio-materials, and bio-based chemicals[cite: 113, 114, 115, 117, 166, 216].

## 🛠 Installation & Setup
1.  **Clone the Repo:**
    ```bash
    git clone [https://github.com/vishal-vetcha/FreshConnect_SIH_GrandFinale.git](https://github.com/vishal-vetcha/FreshConnect_SIH_GrandFinale.git)
    ```
2.  **Get Dependencies:**
    ```bash
    flutter pub get
    ```
3.  **Firebase Configuration:**
    * Add your `google-services.json` to the `android/app` directory.
    * Add your `GoogleService-Info.plist` to the `ios/Runner` directory.
4.  **Run the App:**
    ```bash
    flutter run
    ```
